亚洲龙舟联合会第一届亚洲龙舟俱乐部杯第1号信息公告

Information Bulletin No. 1 for ADBF 1st Asian Dragon Boat Club Crew Cup

致亚洲龙舟联合会会员和非会员国家和地区

根据亚洲龙舟联合会第11次全体代表大会决议，第一届亚洲龙舟俱乐部杯将于2015年10月30日（周五）至11月01日（周日）在中国广东省佛山市佛山新城举行。现将有关事宜通告如下：

To ADBF Member association/federation and Non-member organizations.

According to the resolution of the 11th ADBF Congress, the 1st Asian Dragon Boat Club Crew Cup (ACCC) will be held in Foshan New City. Foshan. Guangdong Province of China from Oct 30th (Fri.) to Nov 1st (Sun ), 2015 Please find the information concerned as follows:

参赛单位    Participating Teams

本次亚俱杯共接受来自亚龙联会员及特邀组织共计16-20个俱乐部报名参赛，各会员协会/联合会限报不多于3个俱乐部，各俱乐部限报1支22人龙舟混合龙舟队。

This ACCC will accept 16 to 20 clubs from the ADBF Member associations and federations, as well as specially invited club(s) . One Standard Mixed Team per Club, up to Three Clubs can be sanctioned by a Member Association / Federation

报名条件与竞赛办法    Term and Condition & Competition Method

（一）比赛执行亚洲龙舟联合会审定的竞赛规则，并有关年龄、国籍等要求参照国籍龙舟联合会有关规定执行。

（二）队伍组成为：领队1人，管理1人，教练1-2人，运动员28人。其中男性鼓手、舵手各1人，女性鼓手、舵手各1人，男性划手10人，女性划手10人，替补4人，性别不限。

（三）各俱乐部必须参加22人龙舟混合组100米、500米直道赛和12人龙舟男子组、女子组100米、200米直道赛。

（四）比赛采用坐姿划行。

（五）各队运动员比赛服装颜色、样式一致。

（六）各队自备1面队旗，呈直角三角形，高1米，宽2米，颜色不限，旗上印有XXXXX龙舟俱乐部龙舟队。

（七）比赛的方法和抽签

 1.比赛设4条赛道，采取预赛、复赛、半决赛、排位赛、小决赛、决赛的方式进行。

 2.预赛分组抽签在领队、教练、裁判长联席会进行。

 3.由于比赛场地为自然水域，比赛的赛道、舟号将于赛前在检录处抽签决定。

i.ADBF Rules of Racing will be adopt Requirements concerning age, nationality will be in accordance with that of IDBF Competition Regulations

ii.Team Composition Team is consisted of: 1 Team Leader, 1 Team Manager,1 to 2 Coaches, 28 Athletes.Athletes include 1 male drummer,1 male helm, 1 female drummer, 1 female helm, 10 male paddlers, 10 female paddlers, and 4 reserves of any sex.

iii.Each club must enter all the 6 events of the ACCC, i.e. Standard Boat Mixed 100m, 500m Straight Race, Small Boat Men & Women 100m, 200m Straight Race

iv.Paddling Position: sitting style only.

v.Athletes' racing jerseys should be in the same color and design within each team.

vi.Each team should bring 1 club flag with club name and logo, right triangle in shape,1m in height, 2m in width, no limitation on flag color

vii.Competition Method and Lane Draw:

1.4 lanes will be set in the racing course. Heat, Repechage. Semi-Final, Tail Race,Minor Final, Grand Final will be conducted.

2.The lane draws are processed during the team leader, coach and race official jointmeeting.

3.Due to the racing course locates in natural water area, the lanes and the boat number will be determined by lane-draw prior to the each race at the marshaling area.

运动员资格    Eligibility

（一）参赛运动员须经医院当年检查证明身体健康，具有着衣游泳200米以上游泳技术。

（二）参赛运动员应在其所在国家/地区自行办理户外人身意外保险，运动员须穿救生衣参加比赛（亦可由领队向大会 提出申请不穿救生衣参加比赛，安全责任由参赛队自行负担）。

i.The athletes should provide a healthy certificate issued by hospital and can swim with clothes on for at least 200m.

ii.The athletes should have purchased the Accidental Insurance in their respective countries and regions, and should wear life vest while racing. (Those who wish not to wear life vest, should apply in written, and bear all the responsibility thereafter)

录取名次与奖励

  • 各单项录取前3名颁发奖牌和证书。

    Top 3 crews of each racing distance will be awarded medals and certificates

  • 总成绩录取前6名颁发奖杯和证书，第一名获得“亚洲龙舟俱乐部冠军杯”。

    Top 6 crews of overall ranking will be awarded trophy and certificates Champion will be awarded "Asian Club Crew Champion Cup"

  • 积分办法：将各项目积分相加，第1名积17分，第2名积15分，第三名积14分，......，依此类推，第16名积1分。如各项积分相等，500米成绩优胜的队伍列前。

    iii. Points will be given according to the ranking in each racing distance. 1st will be 17
    points. 2nd 15, 3rd 14, and so on. 16th 1 point. In case of a tie in
    the point table. 500m winner will be in higher ranking.

仲裁与裁判    Jury and Race Officials

（一）比赛仲裁由亚洲龙舟联合会统一选派，裁判长由各会员协会推荐1人，经报亚龙联执委会批准后公布，辅助裁判由承办国家/地区协会选派。

（二）各队如进行申诉，按规则要求进行处理。

Jury, Technical Delegate, Chief Official, Assist Chief Officials are appointed by the ADBF, the name list of Race Officials shall be approved by the ADBF Executive Committee, from the application from ADBF Members. Each member can propose 1 race official. Local race officials shall be nominated by the hosting Association / Federation.
